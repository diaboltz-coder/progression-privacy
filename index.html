Introduction

Progression ("we", "our", or "us") is a fitness tracking mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app. By using Progression, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and password (securely hashed by Firebase Authentication). Fitness Data: Workout logs, exercise details, sets, reps, weights, body measurements (weight, height, body part measurements), and fitness goals you choose to track. Profile Photo: If you upload a profile photo, it is stored securely in our database. App Preferences: Theme preference (dark/light), unit preference (metric/imperial), notification settings. These are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

• Provide and personalise your fitness tracking experience • Display your workout history, progress charts, and statistics • Show community workout plans (your display name appears as the creator if you share a plan) • Send local notification reminders (if enabled by you) • Improve app functionality and fix bugs

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore database and Firebase Authentication). All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS. Firestore security rules ensure that only you can read and modify your personal data. Community workout plans are readable by all authenticated users.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. The only data visible to other users is workout plans you choose to publish to the community (plan name, exercises, and your display name). Your email, workout logs, body measurements, and other personal data are never shared.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services: • Firebase Authentication — for secure sign-in and account management • Cloud Firestore — for secure data storage • Expo Notifications — for local reminders (no push server; reminders stay on your device) These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing Google Firebase's privacy policy at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Your Rights

You have the right to: • Access your data — all your data is visible within the app • Edit your data — update your profile, measurements, and workout logs at any time • Delete your account — use the "Delete Account" option in Profile to permanently delete all your data from our servers • Opt out of notifications — disable any or all reminders in the Notifications settings

Children's Privacy

Progression is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.
