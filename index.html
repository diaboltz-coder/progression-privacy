Introduction

Progression ("we", "our", or "us") is a fitness tracking mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app. By using Progression, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and password (securely hashed by Firebase Authentication). Fitness Data: Workout logs, exercise details, sets, reps, weights, body measurements (weight, height, body part measurements), and fitness goals you choose to track. Profile Photo: If you upload a profile photo, it is stored securely in our database. App Preferences: Theme preference (dark/light), unit preference (metric/imperial), notification settings. These are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

• Provide and personalise your fitness tracking experience • Display your workout history, progress charts, and statistics • Show community workout plans (your display name appears as the creator if you share a plan) • Send local notification reminders (if enabled by you) • Improve app functionality and fix bugs

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore database and Firebase Authentication). All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS. Firestore security rules ensure that only you can read and modify your personal data. Community workout plans are readable by all authenticated users.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. The only data visible to other users is workout plans you choose to publish to the community (plan name, exercises, and your display name). Your email, workout logs, body measurements, and other personal data are never shared.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services: • Firebase Authentication — for secure sign-in and account management • Cloud Firestore — for secure data storage • Expo Notifications — for local reminders (no push server; reminders stay on your device) • RevenueCat — for subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat may collect device identifiers, purchase transaction data, and IP addresses to process purchases and verify entitlements. See their privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing Google Firebase's privacy policy at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Progression offers optional paid subscriptions ("Progression Pro") that unlock additional features. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you retain access until then. Refunds: Refund requests are handled by Google Play in accordance with their refund policies. Lifetime Plan: The "Lifetime" plan grants access for the lifetime of the product (i.e. as long as the app is available), not the lifetime of the purchaser.

Data Retention

Active Accounts: Your personal data is retained for as long as your account is active and you continue to use the app. Account Deletion: When you delete your account via Profile → Delete Account, all your personal data is permanently deleted from our servers immediately. This includes your profile information, workout logs, workout plans, body measurements, and fitness goals. Orphaned Data: If data becomes orphaned due to a technical issue (e.g. partial deletion), it will be cleaned up during routine maintenance. Backups: Firebase may retain encrypted backups for a short period as part of its infrastructure. These are not accessible and are automatically purged. Community Plans: Workout plans you published to the community remain available after account deletion unless you delete them first. Published plans only contain the plan structure and your display name — no personal data.
